The Digital Overdose community is running a Rookie Conference !

From now until March 7th 23:59 GMT+1, you may submit a talk proposal to this address: digitaloverdosecon+cfp@gmail.com

Only one requirement for you to be eligible: You must never have spoken at a con before.

Your proposal can be as short as:

  • A title
  • The subject matter
  • A short description
  • Your twitter handle

The first three elements (and nothing more) will then be provided to a blind panel !
