Digital Overdose Conference

(aka. D_OVER CON)


What's all of this about ?

Digital Overdose is proud to announce that we are running an information security conference !

The catch? The only people able to speak there are so-called rookies. Rookies not necessarily in the sense that they are new to Information Security, but more specifically in the sense that they've never had the experience of talking at a conference previously!

With the number of conventions with rookie tracks that have been cancelled, focusing specifically on rookies lets them have all of the well-deserved limelight, and helps foster the next generation of representatives of the community.

Anyone else is of course free to attend.

So if you're free on the 17th and 18th of April (2021), then save the date !

Are you interested ?

So we've managed to pique your interest ? That's awesome !

The good news is that from now until March 7th 23:59 GMT+1, you may submit a talk proposal to this address: digitaloverdosecon+cfp@gmail.com.

Your proposal can be as simple as:

  • A title
  • The subject matter
  • A short description
  • Your name and twitter handle

We will then forward the first three elements (and nothing more) to a blind panel, to make sure that everyone gets a fair shot at speaking !
