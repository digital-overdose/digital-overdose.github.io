Code of Conduct

Much like our general community, the Digital Overdose Conference asks of its participants to abide by a Code of Conduct. This is a minimum set of rules that allow every conference speaker, attendee, or organiser to have a great time.

The Code of Conduct is as follows, and applies to all of the conference spaces (our Discord server, the YouTube livestream, the afterparty stream).

  • No harassment: Attempts to pressure, intimidate, defame, ... (non-exhaustive list) speakers, organizers or attendees are not tolerated.
  • No isms/phobias: Racism, sexism, ableism, homophobia, xenophobia, islamophobia, transphobia, ... (non-exhaustive list) directed at any person -- be they spekers, attendees, organizers or unrelated to the conference -- are not tolerated.

Violation of the aforementioned rules will result in your removal from the Conference by our moderation team.

By joining the community's Discord server, you are subjected to additional rules, which you will have to accept upon joining.
