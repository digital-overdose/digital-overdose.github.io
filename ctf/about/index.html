About

Digital Overdose has started doing jeopardy-style "Capture The Flag" (CTF) events!

A jeopardy-style CTF works by hiding flags in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites, and asking the participants to submit them once found for points.

More points = Better score = Better chance at winning = Better chance at getting swag.

Digital Overdose is an information security and cybersecurity focused community for people of all levels of experience.
